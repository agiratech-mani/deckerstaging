Does this Point of View work?

Listener: Logistics Manager who ships out several hundred boxes each day, all around the world, responsible for on-time delivery of all products to the company’s customers.

Topic: Our company’s services are superb.

Goal: To convince potential customers to use her company.

We have the fastest delivery network of the 5 major global shipping companies.

Not quite...

Why not? A Point of View is not informational. Your goal is not
merely to inform, but to cause a shift in thinking within your listeners
from the beginning to the end. A POV is designed to persuade your
listeners and move them to action. You should be able to state your
POV as a phrase or sentence that encapsulates the change you
want your audience to embrace, adopt and implement.
