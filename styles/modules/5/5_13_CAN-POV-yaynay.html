Does this Point of View work?

Listener: Logistics Manager who ships out several hundred boxes each day, all around the world, responsible for on-time delivery of all products to the company’s customers.

Topic: Our company’s services are superb.

Goal: To convince potential customers to use her company.

I need you to call me back by the end of the week so I know if I will make my quota.

Not quite...

Why not? A POV is not about you. It’s the ask without showing your
selfish ambition. It’s a common goal that we can agree on.
The response you’re looking for is a nodding head. You want your
listener to say, “Tell me more,” rather than, “Can we go now?”
